Retro-Box — CRT Channel-Surfing TV

A Raspberry Pi 4 wired to a Sony PVM-9042Q broadcast monitor that plays shows off a hard drive like real broadcast TV — flip channels, catch whatever's "on air." No menus, no pausing. Just TV, the way it used to feel.
